It's time to rethink your career

As a result of our corporate culture, our approach to work-life balance and employee development, Vertex has earned the reputation of being an innovative company. We offer a collaborative, team-oriented work environment that allows employees to explore original and ground breaking ideas from concept to completion. We understand that attracting and retaining top talent needs to be a priority when you are the market leader.

If you are qualified and interested in applying for a position with us, rest assured that your information will remain confidential and the process only requires a few simple steps that will take 5-10 minutes.

Once you’ve submitted your resume, our recruitment team will review your qualifications. Please understand that due to the number of applicants we receive, it is not guaranteed that you will be contacted directly

If you do not see a position that you are interested in or qualified for at this time, that’s OK! We are growing! You can sign up for your own Job Agent to search our job postings and identify potential matches based on your skill set. It is easy! All you need to do is enter your name and email address, upload your résumé, select how close of a job match you'd like and the frequency at which you would like to be notified. Do this once and you will be e-mailed when a new job posting meets your criteria.
